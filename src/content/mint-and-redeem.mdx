## Overview

$WEUSD can be minted and redeemed at a fixed 1:1 ratio with USDC. This ensures price stability and full collateralization.

---

## Minting $WEUSD

### Process

1. **Connect Wallet** — Use MetaMask, OKX Wallet, or any Web3 wallet
2. **Select Network** — Choose your preferred chain (BSC, Base, etc.)
3. **Approve USDC** — Authorize the Mint contract to spend your USDC
4. **Mint** — Specify amount and confirm transaction
5. **Receive $WEUSD** — Tokens arrive in your wallet

### What Happens On-Chain

```
You: Send USDC → Mint Contract
Mint Contract: Lock USDC in reserve
Mint Contract: Mint equal $WEUSD → You
```

### Requirements

- USDC balance on the selected network
- Gas fees for the transaction
- Wallet connected to supported network

---

## Redeeming $WEUSD

### Process

1. **Connect Wallet**
2. **Select Network** — Must have $WEUSD on this network
3. **Approve $WEUSD** — Authorize the Mint contract
4. **Redeem** — Specify amount and confirm
5. **Receive USDC** — Tokens arrive in your wallet

### What Happens On-Chain

```
You: Send $WEUSD → Mint Contract
Mint Contract: Burn $WEUSD
Mint Contract: Release USDC from reserve → You
```

|-----------|-----|
| Mint | 0% |

Only network gas fees apply. PicWe does not charge for minting or redeeming $WEUSD.

---

## Important Notes

- **1:1 Ratio** — Always mint/redeem at exactly 1 USDC = 1 $WEUSD
- **No Slippage** — Fixed rate, no price impact
- **Instant** — Transactions complete in seconds
- **Reversible** — Redeem anytime, no lock-up period
