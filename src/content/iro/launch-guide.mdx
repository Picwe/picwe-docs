## Prerequisites

Before launching an IRO, ensure you have:

- ✅ Web3 wallet (MetaMask, OKX, etc.)
- ✅ $WEUSD for initial setup fees
- ✅ Asset documentation and details
- ✅ Understanding of tokenization parameters

---

## Step 1: Connect Wallet

Connect your Web3 wallet to PicWe:

1. Visit the PicWe platform
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection
5. Ensure you're on the correct network (BSC or Base)

---

## Step 2: Create RWA Asset

Provide essential asset information:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Asset Name** | Official name | "Alpha Energy Fund" |
| **Ticker Symbol** | Trading symbol | "AEF" |
| **Description** | Detailed info | Asset details, use case, value proposition |
| **Website** | Project URL | https://example.com |
| **Social Links** | X, Telegram, etc. | Community channels |

---

## Step 3: Configure Parameters

Set the economic parameters for your IRO:

### Token Parameters

| Parameter | Description | Considerations |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Total Supply** | Maximum tokens | Consider market cap goals |
| **Team Allocation** | Reserved tokens | Typically 10-20% |
| **Unlock Schedule** | Vesting timeline | Builds investor confidence |

### Offering Parameters

| Parameter | Description | Considerations |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Funding Cap** | Maximum raise | Match your capital needs |
| **Min Investment** | Per-wallet minimum | Balance accessibility vs. admin |
| **Max Investment** | Per-wallet maximum | Ensure fair distribution |

### Return Parameters

| Parameter | Description | Considerations |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **APY** | Annual percentage yield | Based on asset returns |
| **Reward Interval** | Distribution frequency | Weekly, monthly, etc. |

---

## Step 4: Deploy Contracts

The IRO platform automatically deploys:

### GenesisLaunchAuction (GLA)
Handles the initial offering:
- Whitelist management
- Public subscription
- Fund collection

### Market Contract
Manages post-launch trading:
- Origin Forge operations
- Price calculations
- Fee distribution

### Treasury Contract
Manages asset backing:
- Reserve management
- Floor price calculations
- Value accrual

---

## Step 5: Launch

After deployment:

1. **Whitelist Phase Begins**
   - Add addresses to whitelist
   - Whitelisted users can subscribe

2. **Public Offering Opens**
   - All users can participate
   - Subscriptions collected

3. **Market Initialization**
   - Funding period ends
   - Market contract activated
   - Tokens become claimable

4. **Trading Begins**
   - Origin Forge live
   - Continuous forge/melt operations

---

## Important Notes

### Current Review Process

IROs currently undergo review before launch to ensure quality and compliance. We're working toward fully permissionless issuance.

### Regulatory Responsibility

Issuers are responsible for:
- Compliance with local regulations
- Accurate asset representation
- Investor communications

### Best Practices

- **Transparency** — Provide complete asset information
- **Communication** — Maintain active investor relations
- **Delivery** — Ensure APY promises are sustainable
- **Community** — Build and engage your investor base
