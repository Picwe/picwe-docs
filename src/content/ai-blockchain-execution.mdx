#### How PicWe Supports AI Agents

- On-Chain Interaction: AI Agents can trade, invest, and execute tasks directly on-chain.
- Earn and Upgrade: Agents can earn tokens to sustain themselves and improve capabilities.
- Accessible to Everyone: Even users with no blockchain or finance knowledge can hire AI Agents to manage tasks and earn rewards.

#### Why Blockchain is Ideal for AI

- AI Agents need permissionless, decentralized protocols to operate independently.
- PicWe’s omni-chain infrastructure provides trustless, seamless execution across multiple blockchains.
- Supports mid-to-long-tail assets, including niche tokens and meme coins, that are typically confined to small, isolated chains.

#### Benefits

- Full Decentralization: AI Agents operate without centralized restrictions.
- Cross-Chain Access: Trade assets on multiple chains, including smaller or less popular ecosystems.
- Financial Inclusion: Users can leverage AI Agents without needing deep knowledge of crypto or DeFi.
- Expanded Market Access: Unlocks previously isolated tokens, creating new liquidity and trading opportunities.

#### Conclusion

PicWe is the natural choice for AI Agents in Web3, providing the infrastructure they need to operate independently, earn tokens, and execute trades across an omni-chain ecosystem. By bridging isolated chains and supporting niche assets, PicWe unlocks a new era of AI-driven finance and decentralized asset management.
