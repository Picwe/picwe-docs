## Overview

PicWe maintains a transparent fee structure. All fees are disclosed upfront and executed via smart contracts.

---

## Fee Summary

| Operation | Fee | Notes |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Mint WEUSD | 0% | Only gas fees |
| Redeem WEUSD | 1% | Protocol fee + gas fees |
| Cross-Chain Transfer | 1% | Protocol fee + source chain gas |
| Origin Forge (Forge) | 0.3-1% | Varies by asset |
| Origin Forge (Melt) | 0.3-1% | Varies by asset |
| IRO Participation | 0% | No subscription fee |
| Invest Module | 0% | Management fee in APY |

---

## Detailed Breakdown

### WEUSD Operations

| Operation | Protocol Fee | Gas |
|-----------|--------------|-----|
| Mint | 0% | User pays |
| Redeem | 1% | User pays |
| Cross-Chain | 1% | Source chain only |

**Protocol fees:** Redeem and Cross-Chain Transfer operations charge a 1% protocol fee. Mint operations have no protocol fee.

---

### Origin Forge

| Operation | Fee Range | Destination |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Forge | 0.3-1% | Treasury + Protocol |
| Melt | 0.3-1% | Treasury + Protocol |

**Fee Distribution:**
- **Treasury** — Majority goes to asset treasury (supports floor price)
- **Protocol** — Portion to PicWe for development

**Note:** Exact fees vary by asset. Check specific market parameters.

---

### IRO

| Phase | Fee |
|-------|-----|
| Whitelist Subscription | 0% |
| Public Subscription | 0% |
| Token Claim | 0% (gas only) |

**No fees for participating in IROs.** Issuers may have separate arrangements.

---

### Invest Module

| Action | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Invest | 0% |
| Redeem | 0% |
| Management | Included in APY |

**Fees are built into the APY.** The displayed APY is what you receive after all fees.

---

## Fee Destinations

### Treasury

Fees directed to treasury:
- Support floor price calculations
- Fund APY distributions
- Build asset reserves

### Protocol

Fees directed to protocol:
- Development and maintenance
- Security audits
- Infrastructure costs
- Team operations

### Burn (Future)

Portion may be used for:
- $PIC token burns
- Deflationary mechanisms

2. **Batch operations** — Combine when possible
3. **Choose network** — Some networks are cheaper
