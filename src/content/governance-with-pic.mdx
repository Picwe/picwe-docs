# Governance with $PIC

<div style={{ color: 'rgb(110,110,100)', clear: 'both', fontSize: '1.125rem', marginTop: '1.25rem' }}>Shape the Future of PicWe</div>

PicWe uses a fully on-chain, decentralized governance model that empowers $PIC token holders to actively participate in the ecosystem’s development and decision-making.

By holding $PIC, you can vote on proposals, suggest initiatives, and influence the growth of PicWe’s omni-chain infrastructure.

---

#### **Key Governance Proposals**

1. **Protocol Revenue Allocation**
   - Decide how PicWe’s revenue is used for $PIC buybacks and burns each quarter.
2. **Priority Chain Support:**
   - Determine which blockchains should receive development and marketing focus to drive adoption.
3. **Protocol Transaction Fees:**
   - Set cross-chain transaction fees for each supported chain, maintaining a fair and balanced system.
4. **Initial Liquidity Configuration:**
   - Decide funding amounts for $WEUSD liquidity pools on each chain, ensuring smooth cross-chain transactions.
5. **Composition of $WEUSD Treasury Assets:**
   - Vote on which blockchain-native assets (e.g., BTC, ETH) should back $WEUSD, reflecting community preferences.
6. **Stake $PIC**
   - To provide liquidity for PicWe’s application chain, earning liquidity yields.

---

#### Why Governance Matters

- Transparent & Community-Driven: All decisions are recorded on-chain, giving everyone visibility.
- Empowered Participation: $PIC holders directly influence key economic and operational decisions.
- Shape the Ecosystem: Your votes help build the future of PicWe’s omni-chain infrastructure, bridging assets and liquidity across Web3.

**Conclusion**

Holding $PIC isn’t just ownership, it’s active participation in a decentralized ecosystem. Shape liquidity, fees, treasury assets, and strategic priorities while helping PicWe grow as a trusted, community-driven omni-chain infrastructure.
