## Overview

WEUSD is designed for native cross-chain operations. Transfer your WEUSD between supported networks without using external bridges or wrapped tokens.

---

## Architecture

![Cross-Chain Architecture](/omni-protocol-investor-overview.svg)

### Self-Balancing Mechanism

PicWe's cross-chain infrastructure uses a self-balancing system:

1. **Unified Liquidity** — WEUSD reserves are managed across all chains
2. **Automatic Rebalancing** — The protocol adjusts reserves based on demand
3. **No Bridge Risks** — Direct protocol-level transfers, not third-party bridges

---

## How It Works

### Transfer Process

![Cross-Chain Transfer Flow](/images/cross-chain-transfer-flow.svg)

1. **Initiate** — Request transfer on source chain
2. **Lock** — WEUSD locked in source chain contract
3. **Verify** — Cross-chain message verified
4. **Release** — WEUSD minted/released on target chain

### Execution Time

| Route | Typical Time |
|-------|--------------|
| EVM ↔ EVM | 1-5 minutes |
| EVM ↔ Movement | 5-15 minutes |

## Fees

| Component | Fee |
|-----------|-----|
| Protocol Fee | 0% |
| Gas (Source Chain) | Variable |
| Gas (Target Chain) | Included |

Cross-chain transfers only require gas fees on the source chain.

## Use Cases

### RWA Purchases

1. Hold USDC on Arbitrum
2. Mint WEUSD on Arbitrum
3. Transfer WEUSD to Base
4. Participate in Base-based IRO

### Portfolio Rebalancing

Move WEUSD to chains with better opportunities:
- Higher APY in Invest Module
- New IRO launches
- Different RWA offerings

### Settlement

Complete RWA transactions across chains:
- Buy RWA on BSC, settle on Base
- Cross-chain portfolio management

---

## Important Notes

- **Same Token** — WEUSD is the same token on all chains, not a wrapped version
- **No Slippage** — 1:1 transfer, no price impact
- **Protocol Security** — Secured by PicWe's smart contracts, not external bridges
- **Reversible** — Transfer back anytime
