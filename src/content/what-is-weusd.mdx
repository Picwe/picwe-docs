## Definition

**$WEUSD** is PicWe's native stablecoin, pegged 1:1 to USDC and designed for cross-chain settlement. Unlike traditional stablecoins that operate on single chains, $WEUSD functions as a unified settlement layer across multiple blockchains.

---

## Core Properties

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Peg** | 1 $WEUSD = 1 USDC, always |
| **Collateral** | 100% backed by user-deposited USDC |
| **Decimals** | 6 (same as USDC) |
| **Control** | Fully decentralized, smart contract managed |

---

## Key Features

### 1. Omni-Chain Native

$WEUSD works seamlessly across all supported networks:
- BSC Mainnet
- Base Mainnet
- Arbitrum One
- HashKey Chain
- Plume Chain
- Movement

No bridges required. Transfer $WEUSD between chains directly through PicWe's cross-chain infrastructure.

### 2. Full Collateralization

Every $WEUSD in circulation is backed 1:1 by USDC locked in smart contracts. You can always redeem your $WEUSD for USDC at par value.

### 3. Decentralized Control

No central authority controls $WEUSD. All minting, burning, and transfers are executed by immutable smart contracts.

## Use Cases

### RWA Transactions

$WEUSD is the primary settlement currency for all RWA operations on PicWe:
- **IRO Participation** — Subscribe to Initial RWA Offerings
- **Origin Forge** — Forge and melt RWA tokens
- **Invest Module** — Stake in structured RWA products

### Cross-Chain Payments

Move value across blockchains without bridge risks:
- Send $WEUSD from BSC to Base
- Receive $WEUSD on Arbitrum from HashKey
- Settle RWA trades across different networks

### Stable Value Storage

Hold $WEUSD as a stable-value asset:
- No volatility risk
- Redeemable for USDC anytime
- Earn opportunities in Invest Module
