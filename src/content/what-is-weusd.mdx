**$WEUSD** is PicWe’s **native omni-chain stablecoin**, designed for **stability, security, and seamless transactions** across PicWe’s **omni-chain infrastructure**. It acts as the **settlement layer** for the entire PicWe ecosystem, powering **DeFi, RWA platforms, cross-chain payments**, and provides **AI Agents with on-chain interaction capability** — allowing **AI Agents** to directly **use and carry out actions on-chain**.

---

### **Key Features of $WEUSD:**

| Feature                   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Omni-Chain Settlement** | <p></p><ul><li>Works natively across PicWe’s Omni-chain infrastructure. (Ethereum, BNB Chain, Arbitrum, Movement, and more).</li><li>No risky third-party bridges, no custodians, no delays.</li><li>Perfect for DeFi protocols, cross-chain apps, and AI agents that need unified liquidity.</li></ul> |
| **Price Stability**       | <p></p><ul><li>Pegged 1:1 with USDC, always redeemable at $1USDC.</li><li>Users can mint $WEUSD by depositing USDC or redeem it instantly on any supported chain.</li><li>Immune to the depegging risks faced by algorithmic or undercollateralized stablecoins.</li></ul>                              |
| **Decentralized**         | <p></p><ul><li>Fully controlled by smart contracts, not by a central authority.</li><li>Every $WEUSD in circulation is 100% collateralized by user-supplied USDC.</li><li>Permissionless, verifiable, and fully transparent on-chain.</li></ul>                                                         |

#### Why $WEUSD Matters

$WEUSD isn’t just another stablecoin, it’s the foundation of omni-chain liquidity. By combining decentralization, security, and seamless cross-chain settlement, it enables:

- DeFi platforms to access stable, borderless liquidity.
- RWA projects to settle investments securely across chains.
- AI agents to execute on-chain payments and portfolio management.
- Users and institutions to trade and transfer stable value globally, with no friction.

In short, $WEUSD powers a more connected, secure, and interoperable future for crypto and real-world assets (RWAs).
