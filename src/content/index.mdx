## Quick Start

**For Investors:**
1. Get WEUSD by [minting](/mint-and-redeem) with USDC
2. Participate in [IRO](/iro) to acquire RWA tokens early
3. Or [forge](/origin-forge) RWA tokens anytime at market price

**For Issuers:**
1. [Launch your RWA](/iro) through our IRO platform
2. Set parameters (APY, funding cap, allocation)
3. Let the market discover your asset's value

**For Developers:**
1. Check our [contract addresses](/developers/contracts)
2. Review the [API reference](/developers/api-reference)
3. Build on PicWe's infrastructure
