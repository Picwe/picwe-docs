## Before Contacting Support

### Check These First

1. **FAQ** — [Frequently Asked Questions](/faq)
2. **Documentation** — Browse relevant sections
3. **Transaction Status** — Check block explorer
4. **Wallet** — Ensure correct network and balance

### Common Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Transaction stuck | Wait or increase gas |
| Balance not showing | Switch networks, refresh |
| Can't connect wallet | Clear cache, try different browser |
| Approval failed | Check gas, try again |

## Community Guidelines

When seeking help:

**Do:**
- Be specific about your issue
- Provide relevant details
- Be patient and respectful
- Search before asking

**Don't:**
- Share private keys or seed phrases
- Trust unsolicited DMs claiming to be support
- Click suspicious links
- Share sensitive personal information
